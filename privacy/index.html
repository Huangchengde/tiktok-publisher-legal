KnowGrow Creator Studio

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

1. Scope

This policy explains how KnowGrow Creator Studio processes information when independent creators and small businesses prepare videos, connect TikTok accounts, and request publishing actions.

2. Information processed

Depending on the feature used, KnowGrow may process video metadata, captions, hashtags, publishing settings, TikTok account identifiers returned by TikTok, authorization tokens, upload or publishing status, device and browser information, and technical logs needed to operate and secure the service.

3. Local video preparation

The Creator Studio previews the selected video locally in your browser. The publication check sends file size and post settings to the KnowGrow service, but does not send the video file. A video is transferred only when you authorize TikTok and explicitly proceed with an upload feature.

4. How information is used

Information is used to provide video preparation, validate publishing settings, complete TikTok authorization, initiate user-approved uploads, display publishing status, prevent abuse, troubleshoot failures, and maintain service security.

5. TikTok authorization data

TikTok authorization occurs through TikTok's official OAuth flow. KnowGrow does not receive your TikTok password. Access and refresh tokens, when issued, are treated as confidential credentials and are used only for the authorized account, approved scopes, and requested service actions.

6. Sharing

Content and metadata may be sent to TikTok when you explicitly approve an upload or publication action. Information is not sold. Limited information may be processed by service providers that host the website or API, or disclosed when required by law, security, fraud prevention, or protection of users and systems.

7. Retention

Information is retained only as long as reasonably needed for the requested service, security, troubleshooting, legal compliance, or resolving disputes. Locally selected browser videos are not retained by the publication check. Retention periods may vary by record type and applicable obligations.

8. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used to protect authorization data, media, and publishing records. No transmission or storage system is completely secure, so users should avoid submitting unnecessary sensitive information.

9. Your choices

You may stop using the service, revoke TikTok authorization through TikTok controls, clear local browser data, or request deletion of eligible KnowGrow records. See the Data deletion page for instructions.

10. Children

KnowGrow is not directed to children below the age required to consent to online services in their jurisdiction. Do not use the service if you are not legally eligible to authorize a TikTok account and accept these terms.

11. Changes

This policy may be updated as features, legal requirements, or service providers change. The updated date will be shown at the top of this page.

12. Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, follow the instructions on the Support and Data deletion pages. Never publish access tokens or private videos in a public request.
