Everything before publish

A clear workflow, with the creator in control.

Know what will be posted and which account will receive it before any upload begins.

01

Local video preview

Select and inspect your video in the browser. Confirm file type, size, duration, and framing before continuing.

02

Publishing settings

Review the caption, privacy level, comments, Duet, Stitch, branded-content, and AI-generated-content settings.

03

Official TikTok OAuth

Connect your own TikTok account through TikTok's authorization page. KnowGrow never asks for your TikTok password.

04

Explicit final approval

Nothing is published automatically. A creator must review and confirm each post before the official upload flow starts.

How it works

Four deliberate steps.

  1. 1
    Select your video

    Choose content you own or have permission to publish.

  2. 2
    Complete the review

    Check the preview, caption, privacy, and interaction controls.

  3. 3
    Connect TikTok

    Authorize the requested scopes on TikTok's official OAuth screen.

  4. 4
    Confirm the post

    Approve the final settings and follow the publishing status.

Designed around consent

Your content. Your account. Your decision.

Minimum access
Only the TikTok permissions needed for the requested feature are requested.

No password collection
Account authorization happens on TikTok, not inside KnowGrow.

Creator responsibility
You retain responsibility for content rights and the settings you approve.

Revocable access
TikTok authorization can be revoked through your TikTok account controls.
