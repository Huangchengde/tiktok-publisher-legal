Data deletion

You can stop future access and request deletion of eligible KnowGrow records.

1. Revoke TikTok authorization

Open TikTok account settings, review connected applications, and remove KnowGrow Creator Studio. Revocation prevents KnowGrow from making future API requests for that authorization.

2. Clear local browser data

The current studio previews selected videos locally and does not upload the video during the publication check. Close the page or clear site data in your browser to remove the local session.

3. Request deletion

Use the support channel to request deletion of eligible account identifiers, publishing status records, or troubleshooting logs. Do not post access tokens or private content in a public issue. Begin with a general request and the operator will provide a safer verification route when identity confirmation is required.

Start a deletion request →

4. Retention exceptions

Some records may be retained when required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or resolving an active dispute. Records are not retained longer than necessary for those purposes.
